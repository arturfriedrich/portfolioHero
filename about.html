Hello, nice to meet you...

My name is Artúr Friedrich, I started learning Front End Development in February of 2021. I am currently a Computer Science student at the University of Győr, and I want to get some experience working in the tech industry.

I started my programming journey back in 2018 when I started learning Pascal at high school, and I fell in love with programming. Because of the fact, that Pascal is not the most modern programming language, I started searching for something new, and I found Python, so I started learning it. I made some projects with Python, mostly videogame remakes such as Pacman, Space Invaders, and Pong.

I love creating new things since my childhood, and I think I can express myself by designing and creating web pages. As a Front End Developer, I can combine my passion for programming and creating something beautiful.
